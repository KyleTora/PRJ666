Ingredients

  • 4 ripe avocados
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 125 mL can jalapeño peppers, minced
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper

Instructions

  1. Scoop out the flesh of the avocados into a bowl.
  2. Combine all of the other ingredients in the bowl.
  3. Mash with a potato masher until the desired consistency is reached.
